Introducing GFRP Bars

GFRP (Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer) is a lightweight and strong composite used in construction. It offers superior strength, corrosion resistance, and flexibility compared to steel. GFRP bars replace steel rebars and are ideal for environments needing high durability and resistance to rust and chemicals.

Advantages of GFRP Bars

Stronger than Steel
GFRP is significantly stronger than steel in certain aspects.
Up to 80% Lighter
Reduces material handling costs.
30% Cost Savings
Cheaper than TMT bars in the long term.
No Theft Risk
No resale value, minimizing theft on site.
No Overlap Needed
Saves on labor and material.
Rust Proof
No rusting unlike steel rebars.
Highly Chemical Resistant
Ideal for marine and aggressive environments.
Fatigue Resistance
20x fatigue resistance of steel.
Tunnel Compatible
Corrosion-free and non-conductive.
